Business Context

Job & Payment Tracking System

A complete flow for tracking every job, payment, customer and vendor in your cleaning subcontracting business.

How the Business Works

STEP 1 Receive Work Order From customer / client
↓
STEP 2 Assign Customer Price What customer pays us
↓
STEP 3 Subcontract to Vendor Assign vendor to the job
↓
STEP 4 Assign Vendor Price What we pay vendor
↓
STEP 5 Our Profit = Customer Price − Vendor Price Track both payment terms independently

Implementation Plan

Development Phases

Prioritized implementation plan for the developer, from core functionality to advanced features.

PHASE 1 — MUST HAVE
Core Job & Payment Tracking
Build the foundation — estimated 2-3 weeks
  • Job data model with unique ID auto-generation (JOB-YYYY-XXXXX)
  • Job detail page with dual-pane layout (customer side + vendor side)
  • Vendor detail page — list all jobs, payment statuses, approve/pay actions
  • Customer detail page — list all jobs, invoice statuses, request payment actions
  • Basic reporting table with all columns (job, customer, vendor, amounts, statuses)
  • Recurring work orders auto-generating individual job records
PHASE 2 — SHOULD HAVE
Management & Efficiency
Power features — estimated 1-2 weeks
  • Dashboard financial overview widgets (overdue, payables, profit, alerts)
  • Aging reports (0-30, 31-60, 61-90, 90+ days)
  • Bulk payment approval and processing
  • CSV export on all report and list pages
  • Payment reminders and notification system
  • Upcoming payments calendar widget on dashboard
PHASE 3 — NICE TO HAVE
Advanced Analytics & Automation
Scale features — estimated 2-3 weeks
  • Cash flow forecasting (expected inflows vs outflows by week)
  • Profit analytics by customer and vendor
  • Automated recurring job generation with default financial terms
  • Email integration for sending invoices and payment reminders
  • Audit trail / activity log per job with full history
  • User roles & permissions (Admin, Manager, Accountant, Viewer)

Developer Notes

üîó
Integration: Each Job links to existing Work Order via work_order_ref. Customers = existing "Clients". Vendors = existing "Subcontractors".
üîÑ
Recurring WOs: When a recurring work order generates a new occurrence, automatically create a Job record with customer/vendor defaults from the recurring template.
‚ö°
Early Payments: Whenever a vendor payment is made and payment_date < vendor_due_date, automatically flag is_early_payment = true.
üìä
Auto-Calculated Fields: profit, profit_margin, due_dates, amount_pending, and is_overdue should all be computed fields — not manually entered.
