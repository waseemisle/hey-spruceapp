Invoice Creation Flow

Step-by-step flow for creating an invoice ‚Äî from selecting the customer to assigning a vendor and setting payment terms.

1

Select Customer

Searchable dropdown populated from Customer DB. Shows name + CUST-ID. Autofills customer address and email for the invoice.

DropdownAuto-fill
2

Choose Service Type

Dropdown with all configured service types (Upholstery, Power Washing, Carpet, etc.). Multiple services can be added as line items on one invoice.

Multi-selectLine Items
3

Set Invoice Frequency

How often this invoice recurs. For recurring, the system auto-generates the next invoice at the set interval.

One-Time Weekly Bi-Weekly Half-Monthly (1st & 15th) Monthly
4

Set Customer Payment Terms

When the customer must pay. Due date auto-calculated from invoice date + term. Drives the "overdue" status logic.

Net 7 Net 14 Net 30 Net 45 Net 60 Due on Receipt
5

Enter Amount & Details

Customer invoice amount, service description, date of service, location/address, add line items. Tax calculation if applicable.

AmountTaxDescriptionService Date
6

Assign Vendor & Set Vendor Payment Term

Dropdown from Vendor DB ‚Äî select which subcontractor performed/will perform this job. Enter vendor payout amount (your cost). Select or override vendor payment term ‚Äî defaults to vendor's saved term but can be changed per invoice.

Vendor Dropdown Vendor Amount Profit = Customer Amt ‚àí Vendor Amt
VENDOR PAYMENT TERM (per invoice override)
Net 7 Net 14 Net 30 Net 45 Net 60 Due on Completion

üí° Defaults to vendor's saved payment term. Vendor payout due date = service completion date + selected vendor payment term.

7

Map Vendor Invoice

When the vendor sends you their invoice for the job, record it here. This can be done at creation time or later when the vendor invoice arrives. One vendor invoice may cover multiple jobs ‚Äî system supports many-to-many mapping.

Vendor Invoice # Vendor Invoice Date Vendor Invoice Amount Received Date
VENDOR INVOICE MAPPING OPTIONS
Option A ‚Äî 1:1 Mapping: One vendor invoice per job. Enter vendor invoice # directly on this invoice.
Option B ‚Äî 1:Many Mapping: One vendor invoice covers multiple jobs. Create vendor invoice separately, then link multiple jobs to it.
Option C ‚Äî Map Later: Skip at creation. When vendor invoice arrives, go to Vendor Invoices section and map it against jobs.
üí° Vendor invoice amount should match vendor_amount on the job(s). System flags mismatches for reconciliation.
8

Review & Create Invoice

Preview invoice with all details. Generate PDF. Option to email directly to customer. Invoice status set to "DRAFT" or "SENT".

PreviewPDFEmailSave

üìå Accrual Logic for Dev:

Revenue recognized on payment received date. Expenses recognized when vendor is actually paid out.
